# Marketing Kit — 12 months of copy-paste content

"Post weekly" fails without templates. Everything below is fill-in-the-blank;
photo direction in *italics*. Always link with UTM so GA4 shows what worked:
`https://smallcakesclarksville.com/?utm_source=facebook&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=NAME`
(change `source` to `gbp` for Google posts, `campaign` per post).

## Google Business Profile / Facebook post skeletons

1. **Flavor drop (monthly)** — "🧁 [FLAVOR] just dropped — here through [MONTH] only. When they're gone, they're gone. Order ahead: [UTM link]" — *close-up of the new flavor, natural light, pink box edge visible.*
2. **Fresh-this-morning (any slow week)** — "This morning's bake, boxed by 10am. Which one's yours? [UTM link]" — *full case shot, taken at open.*
3. **Birthday season** — "Planning a birthday? Custom cakes and smash cakes need just 48–72 hours. Tell us the theme: [custom-cakes UTM link]" — *smash cake or themed dozen.*
4. **Fort Campbell homecoming** — "Welcome home starts sweet. Homecoming and promotion celebrations — dozens, towers, custom cakes. [custom-cakes UTM link]" — *red/white/blue decorated dozen.*
5. **APSU game day** — "Let's Go Peay! Game-day dozens in Govs red — grab a box before kickoff. [UTM link]" — *red-frosted cupcakes.*
6. **Teacher/classroom** — "Minis by the classroom dozen — CMCSS parties and last-day celebrations sorted. [catering UTM link]" — *tray of minis.*
7. **Office** — "Make Monday optional-attendance-optional. Office boxes, labeled and ready. [catering UTM link]" — *pink box open on a desk.*
8. **Gift card (payday weeks + Nov/Dec)** — "Far from Clarksville? Send home a taste of Madison Street — eGift cards land in minutes. [gift card link]" — *gift card art / pink box with bow.*
9. **Review thank-you (monthly)** — "This made our whole week 🥹 [screenshot quote]. Thank you, Clarksville." — *screenshot of the review.*
10. **Behind the counter** — "The 6am shift: [NUMBER] dozen frosted before the doors open. Come see why. " — *hands frosting, flour everywhere.*
11. **Seasonal event tie-in** — "See you at [Riverfest/Oktoberfest/Fright on Franklin]! Pre-order pickup for the weekend: [UTM link]" — *storefront or event-themed dozen.*
12. **Holiday hours** — "Holiday hours: [DATES/TIMES]. Pre-orders close [DATE] — don't wait on grandma's dozen. [UTM link]" — *festive case shot.*

## Flavor Calendar email (monthly — Square Marketing)

Subject options: "🧁 [MONTH]'s flavor is here" · "New this month: [FLAVOR]" ·
"[FLAVOR] has entered the building"

Body skeleton: hero photo → "This month's rotating flavor: **[FLAVOR]** —
[one-line description]. Here through [END DATE]." → Order button (Square
link) → one dated local hook ("APSU graduation is [DATE] — custom cake
slots are filling") → footer: hours, address, unsubscribe.

Cadence: 1 email/month, first week. Never more without asking the list.

## The rhythm (minimum viable marketing)

- **Weekly:** one GBP post + same content to Facebook (skeletons above, rotate).
- **Monthly:** flavor-drop post + Flavor Calendar email + review thank-you.
- **Quarterly:** refresh 5 GBP photos; check listings (Yelp/Apple/Bing) for drift.
- **January:** refresh the EVENTS list in `site.js` for the new year (5-minute job).
