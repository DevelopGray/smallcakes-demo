03 — The studio

How a custom order comes together

  1. 01

    Consultation

    Share your date, guest count and vision — by appointment or a quiet note through the form.

  2. 02

    Design & quote

    We compose flavors, tiers and finish, then send a detailed quote for your approval.

  3. 03

    Approve & reserve

    Refine the details, approve the design, and reserve your date with payment.

  4. 04

    Collection

    Collect on Madison Street, or arrange delivery and setup for your venue.

Most custom orders are composed with 48–72 hours' notice; tiered cakes about a week.
